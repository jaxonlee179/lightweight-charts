Defines margins of the price scale.

## Properties

### bottom

• **bottom**: `number`

Bottom margin in percentages. Must be greater or equal to 0 and less than 100.

___

### top

• **top**: `number`

Top margin in percentages. Must be greater or equal to 0 and less than 100.
